A Sleep You Can't Deny

Imagine a sickness where millions of people fell into a deep, deep, deep sleep from which they could never wake up from. From the lucky few that did, we know that they were reminded of being trapped in a cage, not being able to move or talk, but they are fully aware of what was going on around them. This sickness came and went all across the globe, affecting millions in just a few decades. Those who survived this terrible epidemic were then forgotten, its trace unheard of, so many questions were left unanswered.

When Your Head Hits the Pillow

Encephalitis Lethargica was an epidemic known as a "sleepy sickness" which hit millions during the early 1910's and 20's, and very rare cases have come up since the 1940's. This epidemic was probably the cause of the idea of sleep being extremely important to your health came from, and even alowed of the revolution of the fields of neurology, psychiaty and froced physicians to examine where the brain ends and the mind begins.
